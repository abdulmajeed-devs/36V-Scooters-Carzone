Meet Our Authors

The Experts Behind Carzone Scooters

Our team consists of passionate electric scooter enthusiasts, technical experts, and experienced writers who are dedicated to providing you with accurate, comprehensive, and unbiased information about electric transportation.

Michael Edwards

Senior Editor & Scooter Specialist

Michael has been riding and reviewing electric scooters for over 7 years. With a background in mechanical engineering and a passion for sustainable transportation, he brings technical expertise and real-world experience to every article. Michael has tested over 100 different scooter models and specializes in performance analysis and safety evaluations.

Areas of Expertise:

  • Technical specifications and performance testing
  • Battery technology and range optimization
  • Safety standards and regulations
  • Urban mobility solutions

Sarah Rodriguez

Contributing Writer & Commuter Advocate

Sarah is an urban commuter who has been using electric scooters as her primary mode of transportation for 5 years. She focuses on practical, everyday riding experiences and writes comprehensive buying guides for different user needs. Her articles help readers understand how electric scooters can fit into their daily lives and routines.

Areas of Expertise:

  • Commuter experiences and real-world testing
  • Budget-friendly options and value analysis
  • Maintenance tips and troubleshooting
  • Eco-friendly transportation advocacy
